block level elements -- typically used for content that is an entire THOUGHT or IDEA.

inline level elements -- typically used to convey a term or phrase.



alphabet  --- inline: span, a, em,strong 
words --- inline: span, a, em,strong 
phrases --- inline: span, a, em,strong 
sentences --- block: p, blockquote, div, li, ...
paragraphs --- block: p, blockquote...
stories/essays -- block: article, main, section, nav, header
